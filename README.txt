The Addictive game of PING PONG is here and that too for two players.

A game where you have to hit the ball away from you protecting your castle. If the ball crosses you , your defense collapse and you lose a point to the opponent.

The BLUE color is for PLAYER 1 and RED for PLAYER 2.


CONTROLS FOR PLAYER 1:
W : UP
S : DOWN

CONTROLS FOR PLAYER 2:
UP ARROW : UP
DOWN ARROW : DOWN

Scores are by your sides.

HOPE YOU HAVE FUN.
ENJOY!!!!
